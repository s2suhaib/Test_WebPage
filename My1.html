Service Level management

Service Level Management (SLM) is one of five components in the ITIL Service Delivery area.
The goal of the SLM product is to let businesses track performance and availability targets of their infrastructure components and service desk. It gives businesses a high-level, detailed picture of where problems exist so they can maintain high-quality service. The three entities used in monitoring the service levels are: service targets, agreements and contracts.
